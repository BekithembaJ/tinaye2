Tinaye Maintenance and Logistics

Tinaye Maintenance and Logistics, established in 2020, is one of SA’s leading/fastest growing property & facility management companies. We are dedicated to offering fully integrated Facilities management solutions, Operations Management services of AC, Electro-Mechanical, General Maintenance and External & Internal cleaning to clients. With our assorted portfolio, which includes sectors like residential, commercial, retail and industrial, we have grown from strength to strength.

At Tinaye, we emphasize a relationship-building and true-partner approach with our property and facility management clients. With this approach, our clients have come to depend on their relationship with us to increase asset values, to provide controls and communications and to deliver superior service.

We are driven by productivity excellence and combine proven experience with efficiency, constantly striving for different approach to minimize maintenance and operating costs for our client.
